Orders & Shipping

  • Do you ship internationally? Not yet, but international shipping will be available soon.
  • How long does delivery take? 3–5 business days after confirmation.
  • Can I change my shipping address? Within 1 hour after placing the order via customer service.
  • Where is my order? Track your order via the link in your confirmation email.
  • What if my order is late? Contact support if your order hasn’t arrived 2 days after the expected date.

Returns & Refunds

  • What is your return window? 30 days from delivery.
  • How do I return an item? Start a return through your account or contact support.
  • What items are not eligible? Worn, washed, or damaged items, and final sale items.
  • When will I be refunded? Within 5–7 business days after inspection.
  • Do you offer exchanges? Not yet, only refunds are available currently.

Payments & Account

  • Which payment methods are accepted? Visa, Mastercard, PayPal.
  • Is payment secure? Yes, we use SSL encryption and trusted partners.
  • Can I change my email or password? Yes, in your account settings.

Product Information & Sizing

  • How do I choose my size? Use the size chart on each product page.
  • Are your items authentic? Yes, all are 100% authentic and quality-checked.
  • Will items be restocked? Sign up for notifications on product pages.

Reset Your Password

  1. Go to the Reset Password page.
  2. Enter your registered email address.
  3. Check your email for the reset link.
  4. Click the link and set a new password.

Create an O&M Account

  1. Visit the Sign Up page.
  2. Fill in your details including name, email, and password.
  3. Click "Create Account" and confirm via email link.

Return Policy

You can return items within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with tags and original packaging.

To start a return, go to "My Orders" in your account or contact support with your order number.
